How Hockey Begain


  • There has been many early forms of Hockey dating back to the 1500s in Greece, to avoid confusion we will look at when the NHL was formed.

  • The NHL was formed in 1917, at this point in time there were 4 Canadian teams in the league. Shortly after 2 more teams were added, this establish the "original six" (pictured below). During this season, we saw Seattle win the Stanley Cup

  • Six years after the NHL officially formed the first NHL game was broadcasted on radiostations in 1923. Then decades later we seen our first televised broadcast.

  • Today the game consists of 32 teams with a majority exisitng in the USA, hockey is a rapidly growing sport that most can enjoy to watch or play.
